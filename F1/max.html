Max Verstappen

Max Verstappen is a Dutch Formula One driver competing for Oracle Red Bull Racing. Born on September 30, 1997, in Hasselt, Belgium, he made his Formula One debut in 2015 and quickly established himself as one of the sport's most talented drivers.

Verstappen became the youngest Grand Prix winner in Formula One history when he won the 2016 Spanish Grand Prix. Renowned for his speed, racecraft, and consistency, he has won multiple World Drivers' Championships and numerous Grands Prix.

Quick Facts

  • Nationality: Dutch
  • Team: Oracle Red Bull Racing
  • Car Number: 1
  • First F1 Race: 2015 Australian Grand Prix
