Carlos Sainz

Carlos Sainz is a Spanish Formula One driver competing for Atlassian Williams. Born on September 1, 1994, in Madrid, Spain, he made his Formula One debut in 2020 and has shown great promise as a young driver.

Sainz has been praised for his natural talent and adaptability, quickly making his mark in the sport. He has won multiple races in junior categories and is considered one of the rising stars in Formula One.

Quick Facts

  • Nationality: Spanish
  • Team: Atlassian Williams
  • Car Number: 55
  • First F1 Race: 2020 British Grand Prix
