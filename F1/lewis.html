Lewis Hamilton

Lewis Hamilton is a British Formula One driver competing for Ferrari. Born on January 7, 1985, in Stevenage, England, he made his Formula One debut in 2007 and quickly established himself as one of the sport's most talented drivers.

Hamilton became the most successful driver in Formula One history when he won his seventh World Drivers' Championship in 2020. Renowned for his speed, racecraft, and consistency, he has won multiple World Drivers' Championships and numerous Grands Prix.

Quick Facts

  • Nationality: British
  • Team: Ferrari
  • Car Number: 44
  • First F1 Race: 2007 Brazilian Grand Prix
