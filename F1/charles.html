Charles LeClerc

Charles LeClerc is a Monegasque Formula One driver competing for Ferrari. Born on September 23, 1997, in Monte Carlo, Monaco, he made his Formula One debut in 2018 and has shown great promise as a young driver.

LeClerc has been praised for his natural talent and adaptability, quickly making his mark in the sport. He has won multiple races in junior categories and is considered one of the rising stars in Formula One.

Quick Facts

  • Nationality: Monegasque
  • Team: Ferrari
  • Car Number: 16
  • First F1 Race: 2018 Australian Grand Prix
