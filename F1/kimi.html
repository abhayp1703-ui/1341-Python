Kimi Antonelli

Kimi Antonelli is a Finnish Formula One driver competing for Mercedes AMG Petronas. Born on December 12, 2000, in Helsinki, Finland, he made his Formula One debut in 2023 and has shown great promise as a young driver.

Antonelli has been praised for his natural talent and adaptability, quickly making his mark in the sport. He has won multiple races in junior categories and is considered one of the rising stars in Formula One.

Quick Facts

  • Nationality: Finnish
  • Team: Mercedes AMG Petronas
  • Car Number: 12
  • First F1 Race: 2023 Australian Grand Prix
